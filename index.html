Welcome to the Memory Game!

Instructions

  • Fill in your name and choose a avatar to start the game.

  • Match pairs of animal tiles to win.

  • Click on a tile to reveal its animal. Click on another tile to find its match.

  • If the animals on the tiles match, they will remain revealed. Otherwise, they will flip back.

  • Keep matching until all tiles are revealed.
